About the Project

We are a group of 5 dedicated girls from the Howard Of Effingham school. We are thrilled to share that we are entering an exciting competition as part of a STEAM project, where we will be competing with thousands of students from around the world. Our goal is to inspire young girls like us not to shy away from science and technology but to embrace it. The challenge is to design and create a Formula 1 car that can reach speeds of 50 mph in just 1.5 seconds. In addition to the car, we will be creating our own kits and building a race track to qualify for this competition, as well as reaching out to potential sponsors who will be advertised on our car and kit. We hope you will join us on this journey because together we can "Add fire to your race."
