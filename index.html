About the Project

We are a group of 5 girls from Howard Of Effingham school, entering a STEAM competition to design an F1 car that reaches 50 mph in 1.5 seconds!

The competition is part of a STEAM project, where we will be competing with thousands of students from around the world. Our goal is to inspire young girls like us not to shy away from science and technology
